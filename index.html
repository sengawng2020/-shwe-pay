What is Shwe Pay Token?

Shwe Pay Token is built to make digital payments feel as simple as cash—instant, borderless, and accessible. Whether it’s peer‑to‑peer transfers, merchant payments, or loyalty rewards, SPT is the core asset powering the Shwe Pay ecosystem.

Utility first

Real payment use cases

SPT is designed for everyday transactions: tipping, micro‑payments, subscriptions, and cross‑border remittances with predictable, low fees.

Accessible

Mobile‑first experience

Integrated with mobile wallets and QR payments so users can send and receive SPT without needing to understand complex crypto tooling.

Ecosystem

Merchants & partners

A growing network of merchants, platforms, and fintech partners can accept SPT and integrate it into their own products.

Tokenomics

A transparent, utility‑driven token model designed to reward early adopters, support long‑term development, and keep fees low for real users.
